Mental Health Analysis Project

Why We Chose This Topic

We decided to focus on student mental health because it’s a growing issue that affects many young people today. As students ourselves, it's interesting to analyse these stats because they show patterns we might not notice otherwise. Looking at how mental health issues vary between genders gives us a better understanding of what might be affecting students differently.

Why Student Mental Health is Important

Student mental health is important because it directly impacts academic performance, personal growth, and overall wellbeing. Poor mental health can lead to lower grades, difficulty maintaining relationships, and long-term health problems.

Pivot Table: Depression by Gender

This pivot table displays the total number of students with or without depression, categorized by their gender. It provides insight into how depression varies across genders.

Pivot Table: Anxiety by Gender

This pivot table shows the number of students experiencing anxiety, categorized by gender. It highlights the differences in how anxiety affects students of different genders.

Pivot Table: Panic Attacks by Gender

This table presents the number of students who experience panic attacks, broken down by their gender. It explores how mental health challenges affect students differently based on gender.

Pivot Table: Treatment vs No Treatment

This pivot table shows the mental health status of students based on whether they are receiving treatment. Most students don’t report mental health issues or treatment. However, for those with problems, treatment often proves beneficial. A small number of students continue to struggle with anxiety and panic attacks despite receiving treatment.

Conclusion: Differences in Mental Health

In conclusion, this project shows how mental health problems like anxiety, depression, and panic attacks affect students differently based on their gender. The data also shows that treatment helps many students, but some still face challenges even with treatment. This shows how important it is to make sure students can get the help they need to stay healthy and do well in school.
